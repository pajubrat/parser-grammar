& 11 Binding

	& 11.1 Proper names

		& 11.1.1 Grammatical, assignment possible
		
			John sleeps
			Mary sleeps
			
			John admires Mary
			Mary admires John
			John admires John
			Mary admires Mary
			
			Pekka nukkuu
			'Pekka.NOM sleep.3SG.PRS'
			
			Pekka ihailee Merjaa
			'Pekka.NOM admire.3SG.PRS Merja.PAR'
		
		& 11.1.2 Proper names inside embedded that-context
		
			John said that Mary admires Bill
			
			Pekka sanoi että Merja ihailee Jukkaa
			'Pekka.NOM said.PST.3SG that Merja.NOM admire.3SG.PRS Jukka.PAR'
		
		& 11.1.3 Proper names inside embedded infinitival
		
			John wants Mary to_inf admire Bill
			
			Pekka sanoo Merjan_gen ihailevan Jukkaa
			'Pekka.NOM says.PRS.3SG Merja.GEN admire.VA/inf Jukka.PAR'

		& 11.1.4 Condition C

			he admires John
			she admires John
			
			he admires Mary
			she admires Mary
			
			he said that John admires Mary
			he wants John to_inf admire Mary
			
			hän ihailee Merjaa
			'He.NOM admire.3SG.PRS Merja.PAR'
			
			hän sanoo että Pekka ihailee Merjaa
			'He.NOM says that Pekka.NOM admire.3SG.PRS Merja.PAR'

			hänen siskonsa sanoo että Pekka ihailee Merjaa
			'His sister says that Pekka.NOM admire.3SG.PRS Merja.PAR'
		
		& 11.1.5 Conversations
		
			John sleeps;
			John admires Mary.
		
			John sleeps;
			Mary sleeps.
		
		& 11.1.6 Proper names and subjectless pro-sentences
		
			ihailen Merjaa
			'admire.PRS.1SG Merja.PAR'

	& 11.3 Reflexive pronouns (Condition A)

		& 11.3.1 Grammatical, assignment possible
		
			John admires himself
			Mary admires herself
			
			he admires himself
			she admires herself
		
			Pekka ihailee itseään
			'Pekka.NOM admire.3SG.PRS self.PAR.Px/3sg'
			
			hän ihailee itseään
			'He.NOM admire.3SG.PRS self.PAR.Px/3sg'
		
		& 11.3.2 *Ungrammatical, gender mismatch, assignment not possible
		
			John admires herself
			Mary admires himself
			
			he admires herself
			she admires himself
			
		& 11.3.3 *Ungrammatical, human mismatch
		
			it admires herself
			it admires himself
		
		& 11.3.4 *Ungrammatical, subject reflexives, assignment not possible
		
			himself admires John
			himself admires Mary
			himself admires he	
			himself admires him
			himself admires she
			himself admires her
			himself admires it

			herself admires John
			herself admires Mary
			herself admires he	
			herself admires him
			herself admires she
			herself admires her
			herself admires it
		
		& 11.3.5 Reflexives in conversation
		
			John admires Bill;
			he admires himself.
		
			John admires himself;
			he sleeps.
			
			John admires Mary;
			Bill admires himself.
		
		& 11.3.6 C-command condition
		
			& 11.3.6.2 Ungrammatical, assignment 'John ~ himself' not possible
			
				& 11.3.6.2.1 English
			
					John='s sister admires himself

				& 11.3.6.2.2 Finnish example, grammatical
					
					Pekan_gen sisko ihailee itseään
					'Pekka.GEN sister.NOM admire.3SG.PRS self.PAR.Px/3sg'
		
		& 11.3.7 Reflexives and control
		
			& 11.3.7.1 Local binding inside infinitival
			
				John wants Mary to_inf admire herself
				
				Pekka haluaa Merjan_gen ihailevan itseään
				'Pekka.NOM want.3SG.PRS Merja.GEN admire.VA/inf self.PAR.Px/3SG'
				
			& 11.3.7.2 *Ungrammatical, nonlocal binding out of infinitival
			
				John wants Mary to_inf admire himself
				
				Pekka halusi Merjan_gen ihailevan itseään
				'Pekka.NOM want.3SG.PRS Merja.GEN admire.VA/inf self.PAR.Px/3SG'
		
			& 11.3.7.3 Binding from infinitival into main clause subject
			
				& 11.3.7.3.1 Grammatical
				
					John wants to_inf admire himself
					he wants to_inf admire himself
					
					Mary wants to_inf admire herself
					she wants to_inf admire herself
			
					Pekka haluaa ihailla itseään
					'Pekka.NOM want.3SG.PRS admire.A/inf self.PAR.Px/3SG'
					
					hän haluaa ihailla itseään
					'He.NOM want.3SG.PRD admire.A/inf self.PAR.Px/3sg'
